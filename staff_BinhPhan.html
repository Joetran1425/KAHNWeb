Our People

Binh Phan

Co-Founder | Sale & Marketing Director

Bachelor of Art in Architecture

Bachelor of Art in Studio Art

University of Buffalo, NY, USA,2012

From 2012 to 2015, Binh challenged himself with many projects of different natures: set design for My Village Culture Show ( for both its world tour and residency in Hanoi Opera House) and other musical concerts, art exhibitions and national ceremony events in Hanoi; Two- story multipurpose Concert Hall for Apricot Hotel, the first 5-star grand hotel for Hoan Kiem lake area; Concert Halls for Hanoi Music Academy, Military University of Culture and Arts and Ta Quang Buu Grand Auditorium for Thang Long University; design, manufacture and install acoustic solution for Z111 rifle manufacturing factory, a joint effort between People’s Army of Vietnam and Israel’s IWI, especially the military grade indoor shooting range, the biggest of its kind in Vietnam.

From 2015, after co-founded KAHN Atelier architecture practi ce, Binh focuses on managing and coordinating resident and F&B projects, also leads the furniture design and manufacturing for the company.

From 2018, he took on an extra role as KAHN Atelier expanded, and act as the firm’s Sale & Marketing Director.
